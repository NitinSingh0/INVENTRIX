Beta Sessions

Black box

How to Play:

  • Each participant will be assigned a PC with the screen turned off.
  • You have 8 minutes to write the program on online compiler displayed on the projector.
  • You have 3 lifelines of 30 seconds each to check your code and make changes in presence of a volunteer.
  • Compile your code without errors and produce the correct output within the 8 minutes time limit.
  • If you meet the criteria, you'll secure a spot in the finale.
  • The finale will be held on the second day.

Tips: stay focused, use your lifelines wisely, and give it your best shot! Good luck!

Treasure hunt

Rules:

  • Once the nine teams are qualified, our volunteers will provide clues for the treasure hunt.
  • The clues are in QR format where one QR holds the clue for the next location. To ensure you are scanning the correct QR you'll have to type the password provided in the previous clues QR.( There will also be fake QR's surrounding the college to make it more challenging)
  • The first team to decode all the clues and reach the final treasure hunt box will be declared the winner.

Elimination Round (Mini Treasure Hunt):

  • There will be a team of maximum 5 participants.
  • The name "INVENTRIX" will be hidden in nine letters on the ground and first floor.
  • To qualify for the treasure hunt round, your team must find at least one letter.

Note: Only nine teams will qualify for the treasure hunt round.
